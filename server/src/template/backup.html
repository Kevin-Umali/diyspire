Glowing Galaxy Mason Jar Lanterns
Difficulty: Intermediate
Category: Crafts & Decor
Purpose of the Project

Illuminate your space with enchanting DIY lanterns that mimic the captivating beauty of a starry galaxy.

What You'll Need:

Materials:

  • Mason jars (various sizes)
  • Acrylic paint (various colors including black)
  • Paintbrushes

Tools:

  • Scissors
  • Hot glue gun (optional)
  • Q-tips or small paintbrushes for fine details

Estimated Time: 4-6 hours

Budget: 30 USD

Steps to Create the Project
1. Prepare the Mason Jars

Clean the Mason jars thoroughly with water and soap. Ensure they are completely dry before starting.

2. Paint the Base Coat

Using a sponge, apply a base coat of black acrylic paint to the exterior of the Mason jars. Let them dry completely.

Tips: For a smoother finish, apply multiple thin coats rather than one thick coat.
